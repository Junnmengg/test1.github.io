Frequently Ask Questions




Why Us?

What is DroneX?

Our company DroneX is a company that specializes in curating and selling a wide selection of drones locally and internationally. Founded in 2005, we strive to be a one-stop platform for not only drone lovers, but also drone professionals and businesses all around the globe to get access to top-quality drones that suit their needs with an affordable price, fast shipping, and great customer service.

Wide Selections

We've loaded our inventory with a wide selection of specially curated drones from various manufacturers all over the world. We ensure the quality of our products are top in the industry and also greatly trusted by drone enthusiasts.

Competitive prices

To make sure our customers always get the bang of the buck, we've established great connections with various drone manufacturers in order to get the best prices of the products. This ensures our customers to buy our products with an affordable price without sacrificing quality.

24/7 expert guidance

Our team consists of various drone enthusiasts and experts that are always educated and up to date with the latest news and technologies in the drone world and we understand that choosing the right drone for everyone's needs is difficult especially for beginners. Therefore, our team will always be there on the customer services to help out our customers to choose the best, personalized drone for themselves that suits their needs.

Free return and warranty coverage

We understand that buying items online doesn't always go as planned when the items received might not be what we expected or wanted. Therefore, we provide a 7 days free return policy for our customers if the drones are undamaged, with valid reasons. We also provide free warranty for all of our drones up to 2 years incase of any defects from the manufacturer itself.
